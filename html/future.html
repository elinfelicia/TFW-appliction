Why would I be a good future developer?

My brain has always been geared towards logic, creativity and languages, which I believe are all important pillars that make up a good developer. I have a keen eye for detail and a knack for problem-solving. The idea of creating something new that will help people in various ways genuinely excites me.
Having moved all around the world on my own throughout my late teens and twenties, I believe I have gained a rather unique perspective on life and an ability to solve most challenges that come my way. I think a great team of developers is made up of people from different backgrounds and with different ways of thinking and looking at the problem - a variety of minds examining the task at hand from their own unique point of view, and all coming together to find the best solution. I believe that I can contribute to this and become a valuable member of a team taking on exciting new projects together.
